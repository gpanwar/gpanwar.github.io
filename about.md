Enterprise Network Engineer & DevOps, NOC, NMSU. PhD Candidate, Computer Science, NMSU

I have an avid interest in Computer Science, specifically Networking, Robotics and Artificial Intelligence. I also work with Wireless Sensor Networks and Micro-controller based hardware projects as a hobby. My research interests include information-centric networking, smart grid technologies, security, and applied cryptography in distributed systems and Blockchain based solutions.


I have a Master of Science (M.S.) in Computer Science focusing in Networking and Security from New Mexico State University and a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Technology, Hyderabad.

I have experience working in the Networking field in higher education industry.
